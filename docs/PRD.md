# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Project Name

UniCoFinder – Smart Study Abroad Advisor

---

# Vision

UniCoFinder is an intelligent web application that helps students discover the best universities, countries, and scholarships based on their academic profile, budget, career goals, and preferences.

Instead of spending weeks searching through hundreds of university websites, students receive personalized recommendations within seconds.

The platform simplifies the entire study abroad journey—from choosing a country to applying for scholarships and tracking application progress.

---

# Problem Statement

Students planning to study abroad often face several challenges:

- Don't know which universities fit their CGPA.
- Unsure which countries match their budget.
- Cannot easily find scholarships.
- Difficult to compare universities.
- Don't know admission requirements.
- Confused about tuition and living costs.
- Miss important deadlines.

Current solutions require visiting multiple websites.

UniCoFinder combines everything into one platform.

---

# Goals

Build a modern MERN stack application that

- Recommends universities.
- Suggests affordable countries.
- Finds scholarships.
- Estimates admission probability.
- Tracks application progress.
- Helps compare universities.
- Provides visa and document guidance.
